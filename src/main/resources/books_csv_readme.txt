Books with unique (name, publisher) pairs:
    The Great Gatsby is published by both Scribner and Penguin Books.
    The Catcher in the Rye is published by both Little & Brown and Company and Simon & Schuster.

Books with the same name and author but different publishers:
    As mentioned above, the books by F. Scott Fitzgerald and J.D. Salinger have multiple publishers.

Books with stock of 0:
    Pride and Prejudice and The Catcher in the Rye (Simon & Schuster edition) have a stock of 0.
